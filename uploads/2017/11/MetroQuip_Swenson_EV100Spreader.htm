EV100 Stock V-Box Spreader

EV 100 Stockbox
Zoom image

Highlights

  • The industry standard in V-boxes
  • Designed for 20,000 GVW Trucks
  • 25:1 gearbox handles most types of materials, including salt, sand, and mixes
  • 6 tooth sprocket with 1 ½” drive shaft for smooth conveyance of material
  • Swing up spinner system with 18” spinner disk for consistent spreading
  • Self Cleaning style pintle chain with ¼” thick cross bar
  • Inverted V included to reduce weight on the conveyor
  • Top screen breaks up material to minimize damage to drag chain

Equipment

Size Specifications: 9' to 14'
Capacity: 4.9 to 11.2 cu yds
Construction: Stainless or Carbon steel
Application Rate: 4' to 40' spread pattern
Steel Gauges: 10 gauge (12 gauge for stainless) sides and ends with 7 gauge longitudinals
Safety Features: Drop N Loc top screens
Warranty Details: 1 year
Options: Observation platform, cab shield, extended idler adjustment, extended idler grease tubes, pre-wet systems, belt over chain, leg stands, and many more

Materials

Rock Salt • Sand • Salt / Sand Mix • 3/8 Chips • Magnesium Chloride • Clear Path Ice Melt • Treated Rock Salt
